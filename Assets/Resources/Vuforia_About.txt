The Frame Markers sample shows how to detect a Frame Marker and render a simple 3D object on top.

Key Functionality
- Detection and tracking of multiple Frame Markers
- Manage camera functions: flash and continuous autofocus

Targets
https://developer.vuforia.com/sites/default/files/sample-apps/targets/markers.pdf

Instructions
- Point camera at target to view
- Single tap to focus
- Double tap to access options menu
